About YUGMA

YUGMA 2021 is a collective techno-cultural event of eleven ASME Student Sections across Kerala collaborating for the first time in solidarity with the social obligation and team spirit they possess.

The three-day-long technical extravaganza aims to build a strong structural student network and expand the knowledge curve by migrating knowledge, conceptualizing innovations, and creating vibrant experiences for students and early-career engineers.

As our tagline goes, YUGMA 2021 is an embellished ensemble of passion-driven volunteers putting their heart and soul into a celebration of learning.
