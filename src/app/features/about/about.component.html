About YUGMA

YUGMA 2021 is an collective effort of 11 ASME student section across kerala aiming to build a strong structural student network and to expand knowledge curve by infusing both creative and technical learning

This extravagance also creates a platform for budding engineering to interact with technocrats and various industries and encourage them to apply their engineering knowledge in a purpose oriented manner.
